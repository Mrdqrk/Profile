About me


Growing up as a Jamaican American, I was captivated by the endless possibilities of technology. From the intricate algorithms powering our favorite apps to the awe-inspiring innovations shaping our future, I found myself drawn to the world of tech like a moth to a flame. But amidst my love affair with all things digital, I never forgot my deep connection to nature. While I immersed myself in the ever-evolving landscape of technology, from AI to cybersecurity, I also found solace in the simplicity and beauty of the natural world. Whether it was marveling at the complexity of a spider's web or contemplating the vastness of the cosmos, nature served as a constant source of inspiration and perspective. Participating in the Global Tech Experience and interning at the FamilyService Best Buy Teen Tech Center allowed me to explore the multifaceted world of technology beyond just coding. From hardware to software, from data analysis to user experience design, I delved into every aspect of the tech industry with equal enthusiasm. But even as I embraced the fast-paced, ever-changing nature of technology, I never lost sight of the importance of sustainability and environmental stewardship. As I continue my journey in the tech industry, I carry with me a deep appreciation for both the digital and natural worlds. Whether I'm developing cutting-edge software or advocating for sustainable practices, I strive to bridge the gap between technology and nature, recognizing that each has its own unique role to play in shaping our world.
